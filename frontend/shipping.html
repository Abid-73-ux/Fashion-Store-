Shipping FAQs

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

How do I get free shipping?

Standard shipping is free on all domestic orders over $150. This offer applies to US addresses only.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Please contact us immediately if you need to change your address. We can update it if your order hasn't shipped yet.

What if my package is lost or damaged?

Contact our customer service team immediately. We'll work with the carrier to locate your package or arrange a replacement.
