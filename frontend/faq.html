General Questions

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for complete details.

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. We also offer Express (2-3 days) and Overnight shipping options. International shipping times vary by destination. Visit our Shipping page for more information.

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Customs fees and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Orders & Payment

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, orders are processed and cannot be changed. Please contact customer service immediately if you need to make changes.

Your payment method is charged immediately when you place your order. If an item is out of stock or your order cannot be fulfilled, we'll issue a full refund within 5-7 business days.

Yes! We offer digital gift cards in various denominations. Gift cards are delivered via email and can be redeemed at checkout. They never expire.

Products & Sizing

Each product page includes a detailed size chart. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size guide. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up. Contact us if you need personalized sizing advice.

Yes, our products are designed to fit true to size based on standard US sizing. Each product page includes customer reviews with fit feedback to help you make the right choice.

We use only premium, sustainable materials including organic cotton, linen, silk, and eco-friendly synthetic blends. Full material composition is listed on each product page.

Account & Security
