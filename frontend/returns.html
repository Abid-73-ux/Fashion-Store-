Returns & Exchange

Our hassle-free return policy

Our Promise

Shop with Confidence

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy, we offer a simple 30-day return policy.

Return Policy

Items may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.

  • Items must be in original, unworn, unwashed condition
  • All original tags must be attached
  • Original packaging must be included
  • Items must not have any signs of wear or damage
  • Sale and clearance items are final sale
  • Intimate apparel, swimwear, and earrings cannot be returned for hygiene reasons

Exchange Policy

We offer free exchanges for size or color within 30 days of purchase.

  • Exchanges are subject to availability
  • Original item must meet return conditions
  • Exchange shipping is free within the US
  • International exchanges may incur shipping fees
  • We'll ship your new item as soon as we receive your return

How to Return an Item

  1. Initiate Return

    Log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the item you wish to return and click "Return Item".

  2. Print Label

    Print the prepaid return shipping label provided in your return confirmation email.

  3. Package Item

    Place the item in its original packaging with all tags attached. Include your return form.

  4. Ship

    Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. Keep your tracking number.

  5. Refund

    Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Information

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Please allow 5-7 business days for refund processing
  • Bank processing times may vary (typically 3-5 business days)
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Return shipping is free within the US; international customers responsible for return shipping costs
